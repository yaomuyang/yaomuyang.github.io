About String Use ".equals()" to compare between strings Use ".trim()" to remove the blanks on head and tail Use ".split()" to split a string in to String[]. It is important to use regular expression to design the pattern. ***************************** "-" on head: -? digit: \\d or \[0-9] not digit: \\D or \[^0-9] alphebet: [abc](only these three alphebets), [a-z], [a-zA-Z] "-" on head followed with multiple digits: -?\\d+ blank(like blank, tab, \n, \r, \f): \\s not blank: \\S, or \^\s word expressions(including digit and alphebet): \\w or \[a-zA-Z0-9] not word expression: \\W *****************************
